One of our works at College was releasing an application with Unity which could run on almost everything, including browsers (chrome, firefox, etc.) The result of this was Game Maker Maker, a game done with a team of 6 people in the course of 2 months.

The premise: 
We all know Super Mario's mushrooms, Pokémon's Pokeballs, or Pac-Man's fruits, but where do they come from? Here, in Game Maker Maker, we have the answer! Take the role of an employee at the World-Wide Prop Factory, and get ready to bring to life all those franchises you love so much! Be careful, though: it won't be an easy task. You'll need haste and coordination.

Watching the potential of this game, we released it later at the Play Store to amplify the public. Beeing as consistent and reliable as we did it, we considered a good choice for the mobile market.
