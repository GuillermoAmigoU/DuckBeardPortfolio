Who am I?

I wanna be the person who put silly phrases on NPCs (just kidding. Or not. Likely not.)

I am a Game Designer. What does this mean? It means I design an experience, balance it and prototype it so its fun for the player.
P.S: Accessibility is a must!

A Game Designer must know some things about every role to succeed in creating an engaging experience. Because of this and thanks to College, I have learnt coding and 3d art asset creation (simple geometry!). I also messes with VFX and illumination, and I study Sound Design as an autodidact.

My work require lots of communication, organization and planning. As I worked on my first projects, both proffessional and no-professional,  I felt in love with the Producer role.

As someone could imagine, I am a bit curious, so I like to learn everyday about all I can. No knowledge is useless! Videogames, TV, series, books, everything!
