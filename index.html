A little about me

I am that person who put silly phrases on NPCs (just kidding. Or not. Likely not.)

I am a Game/Technical Designer. What does this mean? It means I design experiences, prototype, and balance them so they are fun for the player.
P.S: Accessibility is a must!

A Game Designer must have a lot of tools. I code/script using C# and UE4 blueprints, and I do have basic 3d art asset creation. I also have messed with VFX and illumination (Unreal 4 and Unity), while I study Sound Design as an autodidact. I am always open and eager to receive feedback and learn new tools!

My previous experience includes designing spaces in VR using Unreal Engine 4 and Blueprints (also Unity to a lesser extent), and working in CERT and Compliance Tester at Electronic Arts, on released titles like Command & Conquer Remastered, It Takes Two, and Knockout City.
