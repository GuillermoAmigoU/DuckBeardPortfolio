About me

Currently searching for a job!

First of all, I'm a Game Designer. That includes sound Design and level Design. I can do scripts and code, as well as some art. I am by no means an artist, but I can prepare interfaces, create 3D models and even texturize some. I have some experience as a Technical Artist as well. I am also learning how to be a Producer.

I like to create fun challenging adventures that go along the narrative of the game. Always looking forward to learn more about Videogames, and their design. Also, I love pizza and the heist genre. Ocean's Eleven, Heat and PAYDAY, are a few examples.

I love Sound. I have been learning as an autodidact Sound Design Theory, as well as Foley Sounds for films and Videogames. Also, having some knowledge of programming helps me to create prototypes and help my team when needed (those crunchy times and ambitious Game Jams). I mainly use C# (Unity) but I know Blueprints (Unreal).
Learning new languages/Programs/engines? The more the better.

In College I have done quite a lot of games, with different engines and programming languages. I worked with Virtual Reality both in Unity and Unreal, as well as Augmented Reality for Unity.
