Timmy is Happy


Timmy is Happy - Save Timmy from his impending doom 


Try this game on your web browser! 

One of our works at College was releasing an application with Js/CSS/HTML in one month. The result of this was Timmy is Happy, a funny Clicker game done with a team of 6 people. The main mechanic is avoiding and eliminating all threads that Timmy encounters on the street while he is playing his games.

I did Game Design, Sound Design, and levels for the title.
