Codename Blood Harvest (Prototype)


Codename - Blood Harvest

Note: This prototype is still in progress.

An Unreal Engine 4 prototype where we want to recreate an asymmetrical 4 vs1 experience similar as titles like "Dead By Daylight", or "Friday the 13th The Game" but with our own spin. For this prototype, you incarnate the "Blood Harvester", a being whose only duty is to harvest and drain the blood of innocent survivors that arrive in the realm and complete "The Ritual". The survivors, who need to escape the twisted realm and the Blood Harvester, will need to investigate, find and fix exit routes in order to escape.
The map changes as blood is gathered and the hopes of exiting fade as the Ritual gets completed. The prototype features a changing map, engaging gameplay, and RNG elements so every match can be different. 
This vertical slice is built as a PC single player only title vs survivors AI. The idea behind this would be to expand the concept to fully multiplayer asymmetrical 4v1.
