Rogue One

Rogue One

Rogue One is a roguelike game where everything revolves around one single value! You will have to be careful as your damage, health, money and enemy difficulty depends on it!

  • Genre: Roguelike
  • Engine: Unity
  • Duration: 2 days
  • Objective: Creating a videogame in 2 days with 'Only One' theme
  • Responsabilities: Game Designer, Sound Designer
  • Platform: PC
  • Team Size: 4
Play it here!

Overview

  • Slow but strategic Rogue-like where everything escalates from the same value. Losing HP is the same as losing money, and killing enemies gains HP, damage, speed, etc.
  • Blessings, Hexes, and a Store to spice up the game and force the user into decision making.
  • Levels with random set of enemies, creating an endless experience.
  • Different enemies, weapons, bosses, and effects, like fire or electricity.
