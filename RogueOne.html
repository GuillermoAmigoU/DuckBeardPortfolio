Rogue One



Rogue One, A roguelike with Only one stat!


Play it here!

Some colleagues and I participated on the Game Maker's
Toolkit Game Jam 2019, which main theme was "Only One".

Our game, Rogue One (pun intended) is a rogue like game,
where all the stats are the same value. This means
your health, damage and difficulty, will increase or decrease with the
so-called "value". To spice things up, there are also Blessings and Curses affected by the
value. These can give you speed, or fire vulnerability, among others. This creates
different playstyles, and creates a risk-reward style of play.

To increase its difficulty and provide a fair Core loop, the values of the game do not
scalate the same way with the parameter: We used diferent scales/curves:
The HP was increasing linearly, but blessings behaved as  logarithmic functions instead
(to avoid high values ruining the experience), in some cases we needed clamped values.

The game was done during 2 days of work with a team of 4
people. I did all sound and music for the game. I participated to the
concept, designed the difficulty curves and core loop, made almost all levels,
and some HUD elements.
