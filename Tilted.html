Tilted!


Tilted! An Unity game using Vuforia Augmented Reality (AR)


Download the Game application (.apk) here.



Download the Card here.


My colleague Sergio and I, were asked to make an Unity game
for mobile devices for an assigment on our Android
classes. We decided to give a try to the AR platform
Vuforia offers and design a game fromm the ground up with this
particularity. It turned out to be a very interesting
adventure.


Tilted! is a videogame representation of the board game
Tilt. This game consist on a board designed as a maze or grid that has
to be tilted to move a slider into the centre hole of the board. You
also have static obstacles or other sliders that must not enter the mid
hole. Strategy and logic are keys to surpass this challenges!


The game was done during one month with a team of 2
people. It currently has 15 levels, 2 Game Modes, Vibration and
Sound/Music configuration, and Battery saving options.
I made all the sound effects and the music, and I
participated on the Game Design, Level Design, and Programming of the
game. The game was made with Unity 2018 and C#.
It's totally free to play.

Game needs a physical Card or tracker as well as the Android Application, both links below.
