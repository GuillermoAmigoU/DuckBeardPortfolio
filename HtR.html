Hear The Rain

Hear The Rain

Hear The Rain is a mobile audio game fully accessible for the blind. The title relies on mobile features and binaural audio to deliver a complete voiced narrative adventure.

  • Genre: Puzzle Audiogame
  • Engine: Unity
  • Duration: 6 months
  • Objective: Creating an audiogame fully playable without sight.
  • Responsabilities: Game Designer, Programmer
  • Platform: Mobile
  • Team Size: 2
Download on the Google Play Store here!

Overview

Hear the Rain tells the story of Elia, a blind woman that wants to know what is wrong with his strange neighbour. Navigate his house to know what is going on...

  • Audio is everything: Using binaural audio, SFX, and music for positioning, puzzle solving, and to enhance the experience.
  • Mobile Features: Using vibration, different tactile inputs.
  • Simple controls: Dungeon Crawler grid movement + 7 puzzles to solve.
  • A powerful story: Given not by visuals, but by Audio and voice acting.
